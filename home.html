JOURNEY THROUGH ONE PIECE

Gol D. Roger was known as the Pirate King, the strongest and most infamous pirate to have sailed the Grand Line.
The capture and death of Roger by the World Government brought a change throughout the world.
His last words before his death revealed the location of the greatest treasure in the world, One Piece.
It was this revelation that brought about the Grand Age of Pirates, men who dreamed of finding One Piece and quite possibly the most coveted of titles for the person who found it, the title of the Pirate King.
